Chicken Tacos

Ingredients

  • 4x Chicken Thighs
  • Taco mix w/Lime
  • Cabbage / Lettuce mix
  • Tomato
  • Avocado
  • Cheese

Method

  1. Dice chicken and mix in bowl with taco mix
  2. Cook chicken on med-high heat
  3. Prepare toppings
  4. Assemble :)
